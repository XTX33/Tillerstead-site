Tillerstead exists to serve homeowners, landlords, and property managers with dependable maintenance, ethical remodeling practice, and careful stewardship of the spaces where people live and work.

Background & experience

I'm Tyler—a licensed contractor with more than a decade of hands-on experience in property maintenance, remodeling, and tile installation. My work spans occupied homes, rental units, and small commercial spaces, which has taught me to balance quality craftsmanship with respect for tenants and tight schedules.

Whether it's a recurring maintenance visit, a kitchen remodel, or a waterproof shower rebuild, every project follows documented processes and industry standards to minimize callbacks and ensure long-term durability.

Approach to service

  • Clear scope: Written proposals with itemized pricing, materials, and timeline expectations.
  • Honest communication: Regular updates, realistic timelines, and immediate notice if conditions change.
  • Documentation: Photo logs before, during, and after each project, plus maintenance records for recurring service clients.
  • Warranty & follow-up: Stand behind the work with clear warranty terms and responsive follow-up when questions arise.

Work is done with integrity and care—treating every property as if it were my own.

Licensing & standards
