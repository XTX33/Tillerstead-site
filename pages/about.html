I built Tillerstead to give South Jersey builders and remodelers a tile partner who treats every site like their own reputation is on the line. When you bring us in, you get direct communication with the installer making the cuts — not a rotating list of subs.

Hands-on experience

I’m Tyler — a career tile installer who has rebuilt showers, condos, and small commercial restrooms from demo through punch. My background spans occupied remodels, spec homes, and tenant improvements, which means I’m comfortable coordinating with other trades and respecting client schedules.

Every job follows TCNA and ANSI standards, with waterproofing, substrate prep, and movement joints documented before tile goes in. It’s the groundwork that keeps your callbacks to a minimum.

Approach to GC partnerships

  • Clear scopes: Written estimates with line items for prep, waterproofing, tile setting, and finishes.
  • Schedule accountability: Confirmations before mobilization, daily check-ins, and immediate notice if material or site conditions shift.
  • Respect on site: Protection, cleanup, and client-facing professionalism that reflect well on your company.
  • Documentation: Photo logs, change order approvals, and completion reports ready for your files.

Need paperwork ahead of bid day? Email {{ site.company.email }} for insurance certificates, W-9, or references.

Standards & certifications

Tillerstead LLC is a New Jersey Home Improvement Contractor (HIC #{{ site.company.hic }}) based in Galloway Township. We stay current on manufacturer requirements and reference TCNA guidelines for each scope, including EJ171 movement joint placement and ANSI A108/A118 installation practices.

Tile brands, shower systems, and grout lines are selected with the GC and homeowner in mind — balancing aesthetics with long-term durability. If you have preferred suppliers, we’re ready to coordinate pick-ups or receive deliveries on site.
