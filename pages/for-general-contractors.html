How We Handle Estimates, Scheduling & Change Orders

Working with busy build teams requires clarity from the start. Here’s what you can expect when Tillerstead joins your project:

  • Estimates: Send plans, takeoffs, or site photos. You’ll receive a written proposal outlining scope, prep and substrate requirements, waterproofing details, layout notes, and allowances for trims or accessories.
  • Scheduling: Once approved, target dates are confirmed in writing with a readiness checklist covering framing, mechanicals, materials, and site access. You receive reminders one week and one day before mobilization.
  • Change Orders: Any scope adjustments are documented the day they surface, with clear pricing and written approval so billing tracks the actual work performed.
  • Daily Communication: Brief text or email updates outline progress, upcoming inspections, material needs, and what the next trade should know before stepping in.
  • Punchlists: Work finishes with photo documentation, cleanup, and a signed completion report to make your closeout straightforward.
