Tillerstead LLC brings an owner-operated tile crew to your project, aligning scope, schedule, and documentation so tile work finishes on time and ready for client walkthroughs.

Showers & wet areas for general contractors

New builds and gut remodels receive bonded waterproofing, flood tests when requested, and layout coordination with your plumber and glass installer. Linear drains, curbless transitions, steam prep, and accessory installs are handled with written scope confirmation.

  • Shower pans, foam trays, and custom mud beds
  • Bench, niche, and trim details called out on drawings
  • Documentation package for inspections and homeowner manuals

Floors & large-format tile

Subfloor inspection, prep, and crack isolation keep large-format tile flat and within tolerance. Heated floors, sound membranes, and expansion detailing are coordinated with your mechanical trades for condos, rentals, and light commercial tenant improvements.

  • Self-leveling underlayments and uncoupling membranes
  • Plank, 24x48, and oversized tile layout with movement joints
  • Night or off-hour work available for occupied units

Kitchens, backsplashes & feature walls

Backsplashes, bar walls, fireplaces, and accent details finished with sharp cuts, clean outlets, and matched accessories. We coordinate with cabinet, electrical, and countertop teams to limit downtime for your clients.

  • Layout mockups and approval photos before setting
  • Custom schluter, metal, or wood trim transitions
  • Dust control and protection for occupied homes

Repairs & punchlist rescue

When tile scopes stall or warranty issues surface, Tillerstead steps in to stabilize the project. We document issues, offer corrective options, and finish the work so you can hand over the job with confidence.

  • Leak investigations, re-grouts, and selective tear-outs
  • Takeover of partially complete tile installs
  • Detailed punch reports with before-and-after photos
