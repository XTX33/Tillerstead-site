Tillerstead LLC provides dependable property maintenance, careful interior remodeling, and specialty tile work for homeowners, landlords, and small commercial properties across South Jersey. Every job is scheduled, documented, and completed with long-term durability in mind.

Property Maintenance

Proactive upkeep and recurring service plans to keep your property in good working order with scheduled inspections, small repairs, and clear records of what was done and when.

  • Seasonal inspections: Regular walkthroughs to spot issues early and address them before they turn into costly repairs.
  • Minor repairs: Fixture swaps, door and cabinet adjustments, hardware updates, and light carpentry work.
  • Grout and caulk refresh: Preventive maintenance in kitchens, bathrooms, and wet areas to help prevent leaks and damage.
  • Paint touch-ups: Wall, trim, and door touch-ups to keep spaces looking clean between larger projects.
  • Priority scheduling: Maintenance plan clients receive faster response times and easier access to the schedule.
  • Documentation: Photo logs and service notes for each visit so you know exactly what was completed.

Remodeling & Finish Work

Kitchen and bathroom updates, flooring installation, and finish carpentry with steady communication, written scope, and disciplined handling of changes and extras.

  • Kitchen remodels: Coordination with cabinetry and countertop trades, plus backsplashes, flooring, and fixture installation.
  • Bathroom remodels: Layout tweaks, plumbing coordination, tile work, vanity and accessory installation, and waterproofing details.
  • Flooring: Tile, luxury vinyl plank, and other hard surfaces with proper substrate preparation.
  • Drywall and paint: Repairs, patches, smooth finishes, and repainting of targeted areas or full rooms.
  • Trim and carpentry: Baseboards, casing, simple crown, and practical built-ins or storage solutions.
  • Change order discipline: Any change in scope is documented, priced, and approved in writing before work proceeds.

Tile Installation & Restoration

Waterproof shower systems, durable tile floors, and detailed backsplashes built with proper prep, standards-based waterproofing, and repair options when something has already failed.

  • Waterproof showers: ANSI A118.10 bonded waterproofing, benches, niches, and drain details installed to manufacturer specifications.
  • Large-format tile: Substrate preparation, crack-isolation where needed, and careful layout to reduce lippage.
  • Backsplashes: Precise cuts around outlets, fixtures, and cabinetry with clean terminations and caulked transitions.
  • Floor tile: Tile floors in baths, kitchens, entries, and other spaces, with attention to transitions, movement joints, and sound or heat membranes where appropriate.
  • Tile restoration: Re-grouts, selective repairs, and targeted tear-out and rebuilds for leaks or damaged areas.
  • Failure repairs: Investigation and correction of waterproofing failures, cracked tile, or hollow-sounding installations, rebuilt on a sound base.

Monthly Maintenance Plans

Monthly plans give you a steady point of contact for small issues, routine checks, and minor repairs. Each visit is scheduled in advance, documented, and focused on practical upkeep — not emergency service or full building inspections.

Plans are a good fit for homeowners, landlords, and small commercial properties who want one contractor they trust to keep an eye on things, handle the small work, and flag anything that needs a specialist.

Essential

  • Seasonal walkthroughs of key areas (interior and exterior, where safely accessible)
  • Minor repairs and adjustments (doors, cabinets, hardware, simple fixes)
  • Basic photo documentation and visit notes
  • Priority scheduling for maintenance requests

Standard

  • Everything in Essential
  • Small carpentry and paint touch-ups in lived-in areas
  • Fixture and hardware swaps (client-supplied or as agreed)
  • Grout and caulk refresh in kitchens, baths, and wet areas
  • Light troubleshooting of new noises, drips, or visible damage

Complete

  • Everything in Standard
  • Tile and stone care checks in showers, backsplashes, and floors
  • Spot-checks for moisture issues and obvious waterproofing concerns
  • Quarterly punch-list review and prioritization of small projects
  • Annual summary of property condition and recommended next steps

How the Plans Work

  1. Initial walkthrough: We review the property together, note recurring concerns, and agree on a realistic scope for the plan.
  2. Scheduled visits: Visits are set on a monthly or seasonal rhythm, with clear windows for arrival and a defined list of tasks.
  3. Documentation: After each visit, you receive notes and photos summarizing what was inspected, repaired, or recommended.
  4. Recommendations: If we find issues outside the scope of the plan, you’ll get straightforward recommendations and, where appropriate, referrals to licensed specialists.

Scope & Limitations

  • Plans focus on routine upkeep, minor repairs, and visual checks. They are not emergency services or full code-compliance inspections.
  • Major plumbing, electrical, HVAC, roofing, structural work, or life-safety issues are referred to appropriate licensed trades.
  • Some tasks may depend on safe access, weather, and site conditions and may be rescheduled if needed.
  • Materials and third-party costs are either client-supplied or billed as agreed in writing before work is performed.
  • All work is performed under a written service agreement and/or individual work orders that define final scope, pricing, and responsibilities.

This overview is for general information only. Exact services, visit frequency, and pricing are confirmed in your written maintenance plan and service agreement.
