Tillerstead LLC provides dependable property maintenance, thoughtful remodeling, and specialty tile work for homeowners, landlords, and small commercial properties across South Jersey.

Property Maintenance

Proactive upkeep and recurring service plans that keep your property in top condition with scheduled inspections, minor repairs, and documented maintenance logs.

  • Seasonal inspections: Regular walkthroughs to identify and address potential issues before they become costly repairs
  • Minor repairs: Fixture replacements, door adjustments, hardware updates, and small carpentry work
  • Grout and caulk refresh: Preventive maintenance for kitchens, bathrooms, and wet areas
  • Paint touch-ups: Keep walls, trim, and doors looking fresh between major projects
  • Priority scheduling: Maintenance plan clients receive priority response and flexible scheduling
  • Documentation: Photo logs and service reports for every visit

Remodeling & Finish Work

Kitchen and bathroom remodels, flooring installation, and finish carpentry with clear communication, documented change orders, and attention to detail.

  • Kitchen remodels: Cabinetry coordination, countertop prep, backsplashes, flooring, and fixture installation
  • Bathroom remodels: Layout planning, plumbing coordination, tile work, vanity installation, and waterproofing
  • Flooring: Hardwood, tile, luxury vinyl plank, and substrate preparation
  • Drywall and paint: Repairs, patches, smooth finishes, and color consultation
  • Trim and carpentry: Baseboards, crown molding, door casings, and custom built-ins
  • Change order discipline: All scope changes documented, priced, and approved in writing

Tile Installation & Restoration

Waterproof shower assemblies, large-format tile floors, backsplashes, and tile repairs built to ANSI A118.10 standards with proper substrate preparation and movement joint detailing.

  • Waterproof showers: ANSI A118.10 bonded waterproofing, linear drains, benches, niches, and steam prep
  • Large-format tile: Proper substrate prep, crack isolation membranes, and lippage-free installation
  • Backsplashes: Kitchen and bathroom tile with precise cuts around outlets, fixtures, and accessories
  • Floor tile: Heated floor coordination, sound membranes, and proper slope for wet areas
  • Tile restoration: Re-grouts, repair work, and selective tear-outs for leak remediation
  • Failure repairs: Waterproofing failures, cracked tile, and hollow-sounding installations rebuilt correctly
