Shower Systems

Built to manufacturer specs and NJ code. Waterproofed wet areas that stay dry and look clean.

  • Traditional & foam pans with bonded waterproofing (ANSI A118.10)
  • Flood testing, pre-slope, curb & niche fabrication
  • Linear & point drains • Large-format layouts • Movement joints

Why it matters: Proper prep/waterproofing prevents leaks, mold, and callbacks.

Floors & Walls

Flat, true, and durable. Tile assemblies that hold up and look sharp.

  • Self-leveling, underlayment, crack isolation
  • Porcelain, ceramic, mosaic, natural stone
  • Layouts: stacked, staggered, herringbone, chevron

Outcome: Even grout joints, clean transitions, and long service life.

Backsplashes

Crisp details where it counts. Tight outlets, clean cuts, and sealed edges.

  • Subway, herringbone, mosaic, stone accents
  • Edge profiles or miters, caulked movement joints
  • Sealers and grout systems matched to use

Repairs

Targeted fixes that extend life. Honest assessment and clean remediation.

  • Leak diagnostics & remediation

  • Reset loose tile • Replace failed substrate areas

Important: We specialize in tile/wet-area issues; plumbing supply/fixture failures are referred to a licensed plumber.
