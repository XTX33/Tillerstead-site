Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2025

Tillerstead LLC (“we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting any personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit tillerstead.com or communicate with us directly.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information when you contact us, submit a form, or request an estimate:

  • Contact details such as your name, phone number, email address, and project location.
  • Project details including photos, measurements, or descriptions of work you request.
  • Technical data such as your IP address and browser information (collected automatically for site analytics and security).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Respond to inquiries and provide project estimates.
  • Schedule, perform, and document our contracting services.
  • Improve our website, communication, and service quality.
  • Comply with licensing, insurance, and legal requirements under New Jersey law.

3. Sharing and Security

We do not sell or trade personal information. We only share it with trusted service providers (such as email, hosting, or financing partners) when necessary to fulfill your request. All partners are required to safeguard your data in compliance with applicable privacy laws.

4. Cookies and Analytics

Our website may use basic analytics tools to understand how visitors interact with our pages. Cookies are small text files used to enhance site performance. You can disable cookies in your browser at any time.

5. Your Rights

You may request to review, correct, or delete personal information we hold about you by contacting us directly at info@tillerstead.com.

6. Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy as our services evolve. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.
