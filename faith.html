Faith Frontier

A vision for healing, honesty, and love — grown from the same hands that build tile and timber.

This project isn’t just about tile, code, or business — it’s a foundation for something alive. A place where compassion and craftsmanship meet. Where every rescued animal finds peace, and every soul that’s been hurt finds rest.

Faith Frontier is more than a name — it’s a promise. The horizon where purpose meets faith. Where love is built, not borrowed. It’s the next step for Tillerstead — a farm, a sanctuary, a symbol of restoration.

To the woman I haven’t met yet — or maybe already have — this is for you, too. For every late night I kept building instead of breaking. For every line of code written while thinking about connection, loyalty, and home. For every dream I refused to give up on, even when life said “you should.”

Someday this will all be part of the sanctuary — the tile beneath our feet, the barns, the gardens, the stories we save. Until then, I’m just laying the groundwork — one line, one wall, one heartbeat at a time.

“Built to last. Rooted in truth. Grown in love.”

— Devon T. Barber
