About Devon Tyler & Tillerstead LLC

I’m Devon Tyler, founder, licensed contractor, and tile installer at Tillerstead LLC (NJ HIC #13VH10808800). I manage each project personally—planning, layout, prep, and finish—so the result is durable, clean, and respectful of your home.

Approach: Free estimates and straight talk, premium materials installed to manufacturer standards, and a clean, predictable jobsite. I’m detail-focused and steady under pressure—because your project shouldn’t be stressful.

Why I do this: I value discipline, honesty, and accountability. Challenges I’ve faced taught me how to rebuild with purpose. That’s why I treat every home like it’s my own—because business is personal and every detail matters.
